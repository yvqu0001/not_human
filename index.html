NEW SYMPTOM DISCOVERED

They’re adapting faster than we expected.

Scientists have recently identified a new sign of fake humans. Along with the known indicators — red eyes, perfect teeth, and dirty fingernails — experts now warn the public to beware of hairless armpits.

Like the other symptoms, this one isn’t definitive, but it warrants caution. Tests suggest that recent showering can interfere with detection, so if you suspect someone nearby might be a fake human, take this into account before making a judgment.

Further signs are still under investigation. For now, our researchers have neither an explanation for their emergence nor a non-militant solution. Please remain alert — and stay safe.

RECENT DEATHS UNDER INVESTIGATION

The symptoms aren’t the only thing spreading.

Authorities have reported a rise in unexplained deaths across multiple districts, all sharing one alarming similarity: no visible cause. Victims appear unharmed, yet examinations reveal subtle distortions beneath the skin — as if something inside tried to change, but didn’t finish.

Investigators are not confirming a link to the fake human incidents, but internal reports suggest the timing is no coincidence. Witnesses claim the victims had been acting “strangely normal” in the days before their deaths — calm, quiet, and overly polite.

Our team advises the public to remain vigilant. Further data will be released once identification tests are complete. Until then, assume nothing.

SOLAR WARNING ISSUED

Stay inside. Stay unchanged.

Authorities have declared a state of solar emergency following a sudden rise in surface temperature and radiation levels. Citizens are advised to remain indoors during daylight hours, as direct exposure has been linked to rapid dehydration, dizziness, and the appearance of faint skin discoloration.

Research teams have observed unusual inconsistencies among those exposed. While most exhibit severe fatigue, a small number show no reaction at all — no burns, no discomfort, no increase in body temperature. These individuals are being monitored for further study.

Officials deny any connection to the ongoing fake human incidents, though several reports suggest these “heat-tolerant” subjects display unnaturally steady movements and prolonged eye contact. The public is urged not to approach anyone seen outdoors past curfew. If you witness someone standing in direct sunlight without protection — do not call out. Wait until sunset.
